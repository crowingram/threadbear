Black Forest Naturwolle Trunk Show and Kickoff Event
Naturwolle is a very special artisan yarn that has been hand-crafted in the Black Forest region of southwest Germany for over 20 years. Ingeborg, a spinner, found herself looking for something to do when her daughter left for university, so she took a spinning course. She pulled out her spinning wheel, gathered together some beautiful merino fibers, and made the first skeins of this amazing bulky-weight yarn. After spinning enough fiber into yarn and knitting a sweater for herself, friends began to ask if she would spin yarn for them, too. After completing several special orders, a new cottage industry was born!
Fast forward twenty years to today, and now Ingeborg has a group of women who meet in her living room to create the beautiful skeins that are available today. One interesting tidbit is that there are no dye lots, only spinner lots (each individual batch can be traced back to the woman who spun the fiber into yarn). It comes in solids, multis, tweeds, tone-on-tone, and more, and works up quickly at a gauge of 3-3.5 stitches per inch. A staple for designers for years, we'll have a HUGE multi-garment trunk show and copious amounts of yarn available in the shop the entire month of December. And here's the fun part!

Not only will we have dozens and dozens of models--felted, crocheted, knitted, and more--but we'll have a wide variety of garments (jackets, pullovers, and more), accessories (purses, scarves, hats), baby items (little ugg boots, an adorable leftovers blanket), and more here at the shop for the entire month.

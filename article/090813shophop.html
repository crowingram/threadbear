2009 CAYSC Back-to-School Shop Hop
Mark your calendars for Thursday, September 17th through Sunday, September 20th for four days, four shops, and endless possibilities! ThreadBear Fiber Arts Studio, Yarn Garden in Charlotte, Rae's Yarn Boutique in Lansing, and Woven Art in East Lansing are hosting a shop hop to beat the band.
